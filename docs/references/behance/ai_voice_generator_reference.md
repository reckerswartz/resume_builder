## Core visual system extracted from the reference

### 1. Shell contrast

The strongest recurring move is the contrast between:

- a **dark ambient shell**
- **large white product canvases** inside it
- very soft, high-radius shadows separating white panels from the dark background

### 2. Typography hierarchy

The reference uses:

- **serif-style display headlines** for primary product messaging
- **tiny uppercase micro-labels** for supporting context
- restrained body copy with generous line-height
- dark buttons and navigation text against white canvases

### 3. Surface language

The main surfaces are:

- clean white cards with large radii
- thin grey borders rather than thick outlines
- softly elevated inner panels
- dark inset panels for selected or featured areas
- pill controls and badges with subtle borders and low-saturation fills

### 4. Accent treatment

The reference uses accents sparingly:

- pale cyan / blue glow
- faint white bloom
- dotted / halftone waveform textures
- subtle divider lines

### 5. Composition patterns

Repeated section patterns include:

- hero with oversized product preview
- split content blocks with one strong focal panel
- pricing and comparison layouts built from repeated cards
- inset dark panels for featured content
- floating device mockups over light canvases
- whitespace-heavy compositions that still feel dense because of layered surfaces

## Safe translation rules for Resume Builder

### Reference-only assets

Do **not** directly import or redistribute:

- the Behance JPG modules
- any logos, brand names, or product naming from the original design
- any copied UI copy, screenshots, or marketing illustrations

### Safe reusable ideas

Translate only the **system**, not the original assets:

- dark ambient application shell
- white product panels with large radii
- serif display headlines in hero/header contexts
- micro-labels for metadata and supporting labels
- restrained dotted-wave and glow accents
- product-style side rails, summary cards, and split layouts

## Resume Builder translation map

- **App shell** -> dark ambient frame with translucent header and light inner navigation surfaces
- **Hero sections** -> dark product panels with serif headlines, glow accents, and summary metrics
- **Forms and tables** -> soft white product canvases with thin borders and rounded corners
- **Side rails and summaries** -> dark-shell support panels with restrained badges and glow treatment
- **Marketplace / builder preview surfaces** -> layered white/dark panels that feel like product storytelling rather than utility-only CRUD pages
